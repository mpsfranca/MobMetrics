MobMetrics

Upload a movement trace and get mobility statistics and plots.
Read the paper (PDF)
How it works
  • 1 Upload data CSV / GeoJSON
  • 2 Clean & segment Detect stops
  • 3 Compute metrics Distances, speed, etc.
  • 4 Store results For later access
  • 5 Visualize Charts & comparisons
What you can measure
Space / location
Area covered, dispersion, visited regions, stay-point patterns.
Time
Time moving vs stopped, trip durations, regularity over time.
Speed & movement
Speed profiles, movement changes, trip dynamics.
Interactions
Contacts/encounters between entities (when enabled).
Quick start
Start by uploading a trace. If your data has latitude/longitude, enable “geographical coordinates”. You can also generate synthetic traces with BonnMotion.
